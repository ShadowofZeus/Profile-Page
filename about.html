Personal Statement

Nothing interests me more than the inner workings of software and tech.The question "why" and "how" led me to my career path and pizza and other fast foods got me fat whilst up at night researching. After failing a programming course in University with a big "F", its really funny how I am even interested in coding again. Worst thing about the exam was that it was a 6hour exam (the longest exam I have ever taken by the way) open book - but I still failed.

Pity aside, that fail actually ignited my inner "reactor core" of programming and my first port of call was to do the test at home during semester break till I cracked it. From then onwards, I have taught myself C++, C# and Python. I was then referred to the Odin Project by a workmate and I am really enjoying web development. Thanks Wes!

A nerd by birth and a programmer by passion, my journey as a web developer begins.....I am Simbarashe Masamba
